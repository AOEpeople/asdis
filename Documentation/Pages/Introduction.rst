What does it do?
================


This Extension will replace the BaseUrl to your assets (like Images, CSS-Files and more), to a Domain configured by you.

Example:

**www.mydomain.com/fileadmin/stuff/image1.jpg**

could be rewritten to:
**server1.myCDN.com/fileadmin/stuff/image1.jpg**
